Usha Kulkarni - Managing Partner

She hold Master in Science degree from Aurangabad University. Women Empowerment bieng talked about recently, she in true sense, demonstrated women empowerment long ago by founding Sharda Earth Movers. She represents the strong foundation of the organizationunder whose guidance Sharda took definite shape.

Her strategic decision since inception has helped the organization not only achieve sustainable growth but also build a sphere of trust. She has been instrumental in recent transformation of the firm from Sharda Earth Movers to Sharda inftratech

Nikhil Kulkarni - Managing Partner

Nikhil is a Mechanical Engineer who then went on to broaden his horizon and pursued Masters in Business Administrationfrom U.S.A. He has always been passionate to work for the society and bring about a sustainable transformation. He therefore returned to India after completing his post-graduation with zeal to work for betterment of society through their family business.

He is the execution specialist of Sharda who has vast experience coupled with emphasis on systems development. His logical Engineering approach along with discipline in Finance Management is helping the company to grow in a sustainable manner. Nikhil has been instrumental in building TEAM SHARDA, a team of dedicated, passionate, and highly aspirant associates who work relentlessly to shape prosperous future of Sharda Infratech.

Pallavi Kulkarni - Chief Executive Officer

Pallavi, another woman entrepreneur in the family, is an accomplished Engineering post-graduate having a keen eye for work detailing. She also heads Project Monitoring & Business Development within the organization.
