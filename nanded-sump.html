    Nanded I/A Construction

    Nanded I/A Construction of 4000 Cum sump and 1000 Cum ESR for Water supply to Nanded I/A.

    NAME OF DIVISION

    NANDED DIVISION

    LOCATION

    NANDED-MAHARASHTRA

    To meet the increasing demand from industrial owners in the Nanded Industrial area, it was proposed to construct additional RCC sump along with ESR for further distribution to the industries. Construction of additional sump and ESR resulted in meeting the additional water requirement of the area, thereby boosting the production and trade in the area.

    Project Scope

    To meet the increasing demand from industrial owners in the Nanded Industrial area, it was proposed to construct additional RCC sump along with ESR for further distribution to the industries. Construction of additional sump and ESR resulted in meeting the additional water requirement of the area, thereby boosting the production and trade in the area.

    Services Provided

    • Design, Construction & Commissioning of RCC Sump.
    • Design, Construction & Commissioning of ESR.
    • Dismantling of old ESR
    • Inlet and Outlet Pipeline work along with connections
    • Area Development (Approach Road, Paver Block Fixing)

    Duration

    32 Months
